Welcome to POLA PHOTOGRAPHY

ABOUT

Pola Photography is a photography studio located in Los Angeles that provides the customers the opportunity to have their pictures taken and recreated into vintage polaroids. Our staff provides a sense of professionalism and creativity so that our clients are left with feelings of nostalgia right at their fingertips.
